Who We Welcome

Families Committed
to the Faith

St Philomena School welcomes applications from Catholic families who share our commitment to an authentic Catholic education. We are an independent school of the Society of St Pius X, serving students in Years 7 and 8 on our Park Ridge campus.

Enrolment at St Philomena School is an expression of a family's desire to educate their children in the Catholic Faith and the classical tradition. We ask that families read carefully about our philosophy and curriculum before applying.

The Enrolment Process

How to Apply

A simple, personal process
01

Make an Enquiry

Contact the school by phone or email to introduce your family and express your interest. We encourage you to ask questions and learn more about our programme and community.

02

Visit the Campus

We invite prospective families to visit our Park Ridge campus, attend Mass, meet the teaching staff, and see the school in action. A visit is the best way to discern whether St Philomena is the right fit for your family.

03

Submit an Application

Complete and return our enrolment application form. Families will be asked to provide basic information about the student and to agree to the school's policies and ethos.

04

Confirmation & Commencement

Upon acceptance, we will confirm enrolment and provide all information needed to prepare for the start of term. We look forward to welcoming your family into the St Philomena community.

School Fees

An Investment
in Eternal Value

St Philomena School is an independent school supported entirely by the fees of enrolled families and the generosity of benefactors. Our fees are set to cover the costs of providing a high-quality education while remaining accessible to Catholic families.

For current fee information, please contact the school directly. We are happy to discuss the fee structure and, where genuine need exists, to consider individual circumstances.
