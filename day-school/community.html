Our Community

United in
Faith & Learning

The St Philomena School community is built on the shared conviction that the Catholic Faith and classical education belong together. Families, students, and teachers are united not merely by academic goals but by a common vision of what it means to live well.

Life at our Park Ridge campus is ordered around the Mass and the liturgical year. Students begin each day with prayer, attend the Traditional Latin Mass regularly, and are formed in the habits of piety, diligence, and charity.

Sine Deo Nihil
01

The Chapel

The chapel is the heart of school life. Students attend the Traditional Latin Mass and receive the sacraments regularly, and the liturgical rhythm of the Church shapes the entire school calendar.

02

The Classroom

Small class sizes allow teachers to know their students well and to adapt their teaching to the needs of each. The classroom is a place of serious study and genuine intellectual formation.

03

Family Involvement

We believe the family is the primary educator, and we work in partnership with parents to support their children's formation. Regular communication, parent events, and a shared ethos make this partnership real.

04

The Grounds

Our eleven-acre campus at Park Ridge provides space for outdoor activity, quiet reflection, and the rhythms of school life beyond the classroom. The grounds are a gift to the community.
