Welcome

Thank you for considering St Philomena School

At St Philomena School we offer a unique form of learning with a focus on the Classical Education of the Liberal Arts. We take special care in imparting the rich and profound beauty of the traditional Catholic Faith to all our students.

St Philomena School offers enrolment into all grades, which is subject to availability of positions with limited class capacity.

St Philomena School does not host an Open Day, and School tours are only offered upon acceptance into the School.

The Enrolment Process

Five Steps to Enrol

  1. 1

    Browse & Research

    Browse and research our school community first to see if it fits in with your requirements.

  2. 2

    Submit EOI

    Click on the Expression of Interest Form button below to formally register your interest.

  3. 3

    Capacity Review

    We will process expressions of interest to ensure we have the capacity to accommodate your family/child, as we have limited class capacity.

  4. 4

    Principal's Interview

    If space is available, you will be requested to attend an interview with the School Principal. If we have no space to accommodate your family, you will be notified in writing.

  5. 5

    Outcome

    After the interview, you will be notified of the outcome of your application.
