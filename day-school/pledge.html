Why Pledge

Building the Future, Brick by Brick

St Philomena School was founded in 1999 by faithful parents who believed their children deserved a truly Catholic, classical education. Every classroom, the chapel, the multi-purpose court, the library — all were built through the generosity of those who believed in the mission.

The Parents & Friends Association alone has contributed more than $1,000,000 through fundraising and grants. Yet our work is not finished. New buildings, new resources, and the ongoing operation of the school depend on the continued support of friends and benefactors.

When you pledge your support, you participate directly in the formation of the next generation of Catholic men and women.
