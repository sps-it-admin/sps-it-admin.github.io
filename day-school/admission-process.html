Thank you for considering enrolling at St Philomena School!

At St Philomena School we offer a unique form of learning with a focus on the Classical Education of the Liberal Arts. We take special care in imparting the rich & profound beauty of the traditional Catholic Faith to all our students.

St Philomena School offers enrolment into all grades, which is subject to availability of positions with limited class capacity. St Philomena School does not host an Open Day and School tours are only offered upon acceptance into the School.

Please follow the steps to enrol at St Philomena School:

How to Apply

Steps to Enrol

A simple, personal process
01

Research the School

Browse and research our school community first to see if it fits in with your requirements.

02

Submit Your Expression of Interest

Click on the Expression of Interest Form button below and complete the form.

03

Application Reviewed

We will process interests to ensure we have the capacity to accommodate your family/child, as we have limited class capacity.

04

Interview

If space is available, you will be requested to attend an interview with the School Principal. If we have no space to accommodate your family, you will be notified in writing.

05

Outcome

After the interview, you will be notified of the outcome of your application.
