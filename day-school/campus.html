About St Philomena

Our Campus

61 Koplick Road, Park Ridge

A Living Campus

Eleven Acres, Built by Faithful Hands

St Philomena School sits on eleven rural acres at 61 Koplick Road, Park Ridge, just south-east of Brisbane. The land was purchased in February 1993 through the great generosity of Dr Felice and Mrs Marie Zaccari, after years of effort by Catholic families seeking a school worthy of their children's faith.

From those first buildings moved onto the site in the mid-1990s, the campus has grown — through Parents & Friends fundraising, government grants, and the labour of countless volunteers — into a complete Prep–Year 12 school with chapel, classrooms, sports facilities and a library at its heart.
