Frequently Asked Questions

What Families
Ask Us

What year levels does the Day School offer?

St Philomena School's Day School programme offers Years 7 and 8. Year 7 is the Grammar Phase and Year 8 is the Logic Phase of the classical Trivium.

Where is the school located?

Our campus is at 61 Koplick Road, Park Ridge QLD 4125 — eleven rural acres approximately 35 kilometres south of Brisbane.

Is St Philomena a Catholic school?

Yes. St Philomena School is an independent Catholic school under the auspices of the Society of St Pius X (SSPX). The Catholic Faith is not merely one subject among many — it is the foundation and guiding spirit of everything we do.

What is the Traditional Latin Mass?

The Traditional Latin Mass — also known as the Tridentine Mass or the Extraordinary Form — is the form of the Mass celebrated at St Philomena School. Students attend Mass regularly as part of school life, and the liturgical year shapes our entire school calendar.

Do students need to be Catholic to enrol?

We welcome applications from Catholic families. Given our strong Catholic ethos and the central role of the Faith in our curriculum and daily life, we ask that all families are committed to this vision of education.

Why is Latin taught?

Latin is the language of the Church and of Western civilisation. Its study develops precision of thought, memory, and a deep understanding of the roots of English and the great works of the Catholic intellectual tradition. It is a core subject in both Year 7 and Year 8.

How do I begin the enrolment process?

The best first step is to contact the school by phone or email. We are happy to answer your questions, tell you more about the school, and arrange for you to visit the campus. See our Enrolments page for more detail.

What are the school hours?

Please contact the school directly for current information on school hours and the daily timetable. We are happy to answer any questions about the day-to-day rhythm of school life.

Is there a Distance Learning option?

Yes. St Philomena School also offers a Distance Learning programme for families who cannot attend on campus. Please visit our Distance Learning section for full details.
