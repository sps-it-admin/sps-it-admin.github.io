Co-curricular Excellence

A Dynamic Part of School Life

Sport is a dynamic aspect of the co-curricular program at St Philomena School, in which self-control, respect, humility, teamwork and a healthy lifestyle are appreciated. St Philomena offers a range of sporting opportunities for students to foster their individual talents from Year 5–12 throughout the year.

Basketball Netball Futsal Touch Soccer Cross Country Athletics Swimming

Club Sport

The St Philomena Sea Wolves

St Philomena Sea Wolves are longstanding competitors in the South West Metro Basketball Friday-night/Saturday-morning league, with a team in each age group from U/12 to U/18. For the secondary girls, we participate in the Monday-night Jimboomba Netball competition for U/18s.

Interschool Sport

United Sports League

St Philomena competes in the local United Sports League outside school hours, for students from Years 5–12 — Primary on Thursday afternoons and Secondary on Monday afternoons — across a variety of sports including Futsal, Basketball, Touch, Soccer and Netball.

Representative Sport

Southern Scorpions District Teams

Students aged 10–19 years can trial for Southern Scorpions district teams in a variety of sports every year. We are extremely proud of all the students who have been a part of local, state, and national competitions.

School Carnivals

Cross Country · Athletics · Swimming

Throughout the year students participate in the full school Cross Country, Athletics, and 10–19-Years Swimming carnivals. Age champions and house champions' titles are awarded, and students may then qualify for Southern Scorpions Carnivals.
